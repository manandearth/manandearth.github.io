I remember a visit to a zoo once.

There were some lions behind glass and the path for human visitors went past that glass,
and I remember those kids knocking at the glass, and laughing teasing a very big lion.
That lion could kill the lot of them with one strike of his immense paw..
It gave a big roar, and you could feel the power of that giant.
I felt sorry then, that we are not born of lions.
