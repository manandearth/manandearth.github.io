Thank you for visiting my site. I am Adam, I am playing around with chemical photography and pen art, tinkering with machines and matter in genral, run, climb and hike in the wilderness that I find, mostly in the south of Spain, and trying to form meaningful pieces of text. Here I share like in the good old days the things I am passionate about, the things I wonder about, and the things I am trying to figure out.
