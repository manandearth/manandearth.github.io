About

Posted on May 22, 2021

‘Man and Earth’ was an empty shell for almost a decade, in the early 2000s it was a promising phrase, it’s author (me), couldn’t be happier discovering that the url was not on the hotlist, and grabbed it and paid good rent since to keep it up, although the content changed with his(mine) fleeting love of the earth and the ever changing contract that was formed between the ever changing planet/nature/environment and himself(myself).

The original concept was about seeking a forgotten contract between man and earth. Forgotten due to the detachment of man from nature. Later, more observation led to a focus on the nature of that detachment, what led those ancients to drive themselves to starvation by driving all the mamoths over a cliff, so that there were no mammoths left for them to eat…?

Let’s call this space an investigation into that special realtionship between the two parties, all done with love to this planet, and to the part of the human that is of the planet.
