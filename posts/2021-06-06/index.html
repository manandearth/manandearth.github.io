The Nice Warm Rock

The road is an ecosystem. I know it is a wild idea, but it is true. The warmth of the surface attracting some animals, followed by the animals attracted to the road kill that get killed in their turn.

Roads are criss-crossing every habitat, and the animals are like children without guidance when it comes to roads.

This I find stupid, and yet on the road, society as whole becomes accomplice to the idea of progress.
